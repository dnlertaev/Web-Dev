Recipe 1 - Scrambled Eggs

  • Time: 7-10 minutes
  • Serves: 1
  • Tip - cook on low heat for a creamy texture.
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 2-3
Butter 1 tsp
Salt + pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk with salt and pepper.
  2. Melt butter in a pan on low heat.
  3. Pour in eggs and stir slowly until soft curds form.

Recipe 2 - Chicken Wrap

  • Time: 15-20 minutes
  • Serves: 1-2
  • Tip - add sauce at the end so the wrap stays crisp.
Ingredients Quantity
Tortilla wrap 1-2
Cooked chicken 150 g
Lettuce 1 handful
Tomato 1
Sauce (yogurt/garlic or mayo) 1-2 tbsp

Steps

Step 1: Warm the tortilla for 10-15 seconds so it becomes flexible.

Step 2: Add lettuce, sliced tomato, and chicken in the center.

Step 3: Add sauce, fold sides, roll tightly, and cut in half.

Recipe 3 - Banana Pancakes

  • Time: 12-15 minutes
  • Serves: 1-2
  • Tip - use a ripe banana for more sweetness.
Ingredients Quantity
Banana 1 (ripe)
Egg 1
Flour 3-4 tbsp
Milk (optional) 2 tbsp

Steps

  1. Mash the banana in a bowl.
  2. Add egg and mix until smooth.
  3. Add flour (and a bit of milk if needed) to make a thick batter.
  4. Cook small pancakes on a lightly oiled pan for 1-2 minutes per side.
